✦ FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

Where are you located in Auckland?▾
I'm based in Auckland, New Zealand. Exact location details are shared upon booking confirmation. I also offer mobile services for select treatments — ask when you book!
How do I book an appointment?▾
You can reach me via Instagram DM, Facebook message, or phone/text. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your booking and preferred time.
Do you require a deposit?▾
Yes, a small deposit is required to secure your appointment. This goes toward your total treatment cost and helps me reserve your time slot just for you.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?▾
Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may forfeit the deposit. Life happens — just message me as soon as you can!
What should I do to prepare for a facial?▾
Come with a clean or minimal-makeup face if possible, and let me know about any skin sensitivities or allergies beforehand. Arrive a few minutes early so we can chat about your skin goals.
Do you offer gift vouchers?▾
Yes! Gift vouchers make the perfect present. They're available for any amount or specific treatment. Contact me directly to organise one — I can send digital or printed versions.
Are your products vegan/cruelty-free?▾
I prioritise using cruelty-free and skin-friendly products wherever possible. If you have specific preferences or allergies, just let me know and I'll accommodate you.
